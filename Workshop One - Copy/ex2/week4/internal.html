Subway Surfers

Subway Surfers is an endless runner video game developed & published by Kiloo & SYBO. The player takes on the role of an average teen (or, on rare occasions, a young adult) vandalizing a subway train with spray paint, and is caught by The Guard and his dog. The player must run away from the two, who are chasing them. In the Sydney Logo.png Update, keys (Key.png) were added, which revive your character if the player should choose to use them. The game is available on iOS and Android platforms.
